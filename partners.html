Partners

To bring all the good stuff from concept into action, we need support from our partners. Together as an extended TEAM (Together Everyone Achieve More), they have been a constant help in getting us an extra mile. We express our genuine love and gratitude for all the support.

Moonlighting
A place to work & stay, learn & play

— an embassy for entrepreneurs, creators, thinkers and tinkerers.
Moonlighting is an informal entrepreneurial community hub supporting the diverse endeavours of our members with ad-hoc shared work + live spaces. Tech startups or social entrepreneurs, local or visiting — opportunities and knowledge multiply amongst our network.

Human Circle
Connecting enlightened minds

Human Circle is a movement to inspire and enable young people to do what they love. It is an experiential platform comprising of physical and virtual forums like conferences, workshops, retreats and online communities, to empower people with mindsets and skills required to become impact individuals. The underlying mission is to create global circles of dynamic experiential learning programs to inspire, enable and connect young people to do what they love and play at their highest potential.
It is called as 'education hacking' by designing new ways to learn, connect, experience and grow. Its not just a fancy word but reflects their life purpose and belief system.

Caboom Technologies
Get there faster!

Caboom develops mobile applications that are intended to simplify, bring a change and make a difference in everyday life. It is a venture based in New Delhi, India that aims at providing end to end solutions to taxi operators & drivers.
